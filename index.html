SeeingStone

SeeingStone is a private network intelligence hub that extracts clear signal from the noise across every document, message, and service you own. It maps your relationship graph and visualizes conversation threads across all accounts. It combines AI insights with deterministic automation to manage recurring outreach—like birthdays, New Years, and personal dates—ensuring no contact slips through the cracks. Your data is fully integrated yet strictly isolated on encrypted EU servers.
